Join us for our festive winter formal. Featuring dancing, merriment, and more!

Hors d'oeuvres and light refreshments are included in your ticket, with the option to purchase spirits and libations on site.

All proceeds support our work including our school-based, comprehensive sexual health education

as well as our award-winning Transformation Closet, which distributes free or pay as you can gender-affirming gear to anyone in need.

Come alone, with a partner,

or with a group of kindred spirits. Enjoy a night of glitz, glamour, and general shenanigans in a safe, inclusive atmosphere.

P.S. We are planning this to be a wet event, so attendees must be 19 and over with ID. Keep in mind we just need proof of your age for serving liquor, and we understand if names and gender markers do not always align with your current ID.
